Description
Powerful local anesthetic with a short onset time. Its high lipid solubility provides better diffusion through soft tissues and bones, making it highly effective for infiltration techniques. INDICATIONS Long dental procedures. Maxillofacial surgery. Surgical interventions on bones and operations on the mucosa requiring more significant ischemia and a longer duration of the anesthetic effect. Single or multiple tooth extractions, root canal treatment, extractions with apical periodontitis, extraction of fractured teeth, pulpectomies, and interventions on infected tissues.
